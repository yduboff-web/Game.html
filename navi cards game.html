✦ HOW TO PLAY ✦

  • Both players draw one card and look only at their own.
  • The caller names one stat — just the name, never the number.
  • The other player picks: Accept or Challenge.
  • Accept: flip both cards — the higher called stat wins the opponent's card.
  • Challenge: answer the 3 questions on a challenge card \u2014 you need 3 of 3 right (or whatever you set at game start). Win = a challenge point and the card. Lose = you hand over your card.
  • Tie on a stat: Showdown — lay 3 face-down, flip a 4th, higher called stat wins.
  • The caller role switches every turn.
  • Win by reaching 10 challenges, or by collecting the most cards when a draw pile runs out (tie → most challenges).

The Golden Rule: if you don't know the answer — don't challenge. This is Shmuel Beis. Know it, and the game is yours.
